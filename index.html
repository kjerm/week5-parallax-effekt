Eat more vegetables

If you want to keep your brain sharp, including vegetables in your diet is the way to go.

Vegetables, especially leafy greens, are part of the MIND Diet, which was designed by researchers to help reduce your risk of Alzhemier's disease and dementia. The antioxidants and folate they deliver are key nutrients for your brain.

New ways to eat leafy greens

Healthy food may improve your mood

When some people "go clean," they cannot believe how much better they feel both physically and emotionally, and how much worse they then feel when they reintroduce the foods that are known to enhance inflammation.

Apples Reduce Risk of Obesity

Health benefits of apples

Apples are considered nutrient-dense fruits, meaning they provide a lot of nutrients per serving. They are high in fiber and water, two qualities that make them filling. An increasing feeling of fullness works as a weight-loss strategy, as it helps manage your appetite.
